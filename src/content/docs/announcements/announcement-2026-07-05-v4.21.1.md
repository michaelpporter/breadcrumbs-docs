## Breadcrumbs 🍞

**4.21.1** released today.

**Markmap toolbar**

`type: markmap` codeblocks now show a zoom/fit toolbar over the mind map, so you can zoom in, zoom out, and re-fit the whole tree without scrolling or reaching for keyboard shortcuts. See [Codeblocks](/views/codeblocks/).

This release also bumps a handful of JS dependencies; no other user-facing changes.
