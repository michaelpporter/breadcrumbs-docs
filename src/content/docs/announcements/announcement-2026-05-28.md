## Breadcrumbs 🍞

The Breadcrumbs plugin has a new home on GitHub.

**New Repo**

- The plugin source has moved to [michaelpporter/breadcrumbs](https://github.com/michaelpporter/breadcrumbs)
- All new issues, bug reports, and pull requests should go there
- Releases and the Obsidian Community Plugin registry entry will continue from the new location

**What's Staying the Same**

- The plugin works exactly as before — no reinstall or reconfiguration needed
- Updates continue to come through the standard Obsidian update flow
- This docs site remains at the same URL

---

A note of deep appreciation: Breadcrumbs was built from scratch by [SkepticMystic](https://github.com/SkepticMystic). He designed the core graph model, authored every edge builder, wrote the V4 rebuild that resolved years of long-standing issues, and grew a community of users who rely on the plugin every day. None of this exists without that work.

[michaelpporter](https://github.com/michaelpporter) is continuing that effort — maintaining the plugin, shipping fixes, and keeping Breadcrumbs moving forward. The foundation is SkepticMystic's; the work continues here.

Thank you to everyone in the community who has reported bugs, tested betas, and made Breadcrumbs better. That hasn't changed.
